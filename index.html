My Personal Notes
March 24, 2023

Today I wore my own clothes outside for the first time. I couldn't stop shaking. I'm starting hormones next week with my bestfriend. A stranger called me "miss" at work and I cried in the gender neutral bathroom for 10 minutes. Happy crying. I think... cheers to me being 18.

June 16, 2024

I suck at writing, especially when it comes to journaling. I Scrolled through Twitter for 2 hours again, while I waited for Idris to come home. I shouldn't do that. Every new bill feels like the floor is disappearing. I keep thinking: what if they take it away. what if i can't get my surgeries on time. I can't go back to being a BOY.

August 31, 2024

Someone at work asked if I was "really trans." Like I need to prove it. Like my whole life isn't proof enough. I smiled and said yes. I wanted to say so much more. Today's my moms birthday, i didn't wish her a good one till 11:59.... i wish we.. idk.

Feb 14, 2025

Dating is so complicated. I used to always wonder "Do I tell them before? During? What if they're fine with it and then they're not? What if they're fine with it and then something happens to them?" thankfully, i'm with idris and we made it to 1.5 yr n half.. we had the best valentines day ever, i'm so happy that we're good.

March 24, 2025

Dysphoria hit bad today. Looked in the mirror for a long time. I'm 20 in fucking china, miles away from some sense of "home", and my nervous system is in shambles because the only person I ever loved cheated on me with a cis girl who looks just like me and lied about it 3x. I want to die..
